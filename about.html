The story behind the lens

About Me

I’m an up-and-coming photographer based in Detroit, Michigan, and the creator of Global Shots Photography.

What started as something I loved quickly became something deeper. I believe God created a beautiful world, and through my photos, I want to reflect that — whether it’s through people, nature, or everyday moments.

As a young Black female photographer, I strive to create a space where people feel confident, comfortable, and truly seen.

My goal is not just to take pictures, but to capture who you are in a genuine and uplifting way.

Why I Started

I started Global Shots Photography to turn my passion into something meaningful.

Photography allows me to capture moments people can look back on forever, while also showing the beauty in the world around us — something I believe reflects God’s creation.

My goal is to create not just great photos, but an experience where people feel confident, appreciated, and valued.

What Makes Me Different

  • I focus on capturing natural, real moments.
  • I create a comfortable and positive environment.
  • I bring creativity and fresh ideas to every shoot.
  • I pay attention to the details that make each photo unique.
  • My work is inspired by faith, purpose, and genuine beauty.
