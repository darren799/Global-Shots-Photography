About Me

I’m a 14-year-old photographer based in Detroit, Michigan, and the creator of Global Shots Photography.

Photography started as something I loved, but it quickly became something deeper. I believe God created a beautiful world, and through my photos, I want to reflect that, whether it’s through people, nature, or everyday moments.

As a young Black female photographer, I also want to create a space where people feel confident, comfortable, and truly seen. My goal is not just to take pictures, but to capture who you are in a genuine and uplifting way.

Why I Started

I started Global Shots Photography because I wanted to turn my passion into something meaningful.

Photography allows me to capture moments that people can look back on forever, but it also allows me to show the beauty in the world around us, something I believe is a reflection of God’s creation.

I wanted to create something that not only produces great photos but also makes people feel confident, appreciated, and valued.

What Makes Me Different

  • I focus on capturing natural, real moments.
  • I create a comfortable and positive environment.
  • I bring creativity and fresh ideas to every shoot.
  • I pay attention to the details that make each photo unique.
  • My work is inspired by faith, purpose, and genuine beauty.
